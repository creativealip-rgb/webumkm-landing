# 📋 Requirements - Landing Page Jasa Bikin Web UMKM

## 🎯 Project Goals

**Business:** Jasa pembuatan website untuk UMKM Indonesia  
**Target Audience:** 
- Pemilik toko online/offline
- Penyedia jasa (service providers)
- Travel agent
- Rental bisnis
- Restoran/cafe
- Dan UMKM lainnya

**Value Proposition:**
- ✅ Harga terjangkau (IDR 500K - 3M)
- ✅ Cepat (1-7 hari)
- ✅ AI-powered development
- ✅ Gratis hosting (free tier)
- ✅ Mobile-friendly
- ✅ SEO-ready

---

## 📐 Design Requirements (Impeccable SOP)

### 1. **Frontend Design Principles**
- ✅ Clean, professional, trustworthy
- ✅ No AI-slop (no generic gradients, no card grids, no hero metrics)
- ✅ Indonesian context & language
- ✅ Mobile-first responsive
- ✅ Fast loading (<3s)

### 2. **Accessibility (WCAG AA)**
- ✅ Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1
- ✅ Keyboard navigation
- ✅ Screen reader friendly
- ✅ Focus indicators
- ✅ Semantic HTML

### 3. **Performance**
- ✅ Lighthouse score ≥ 90
- ✅ Optimized images
- ✅ Lazy loading
- ✅ Minimal bundle size

### 4. **Conversion Optimization**
- ✅ Clear CTA (WhatsApp/Contact)
- ✅ Social proof (testimonials)
- ✅ Pricing transparency
- ✅ Portfolio showcase
- ✅ FAQ section

---

## 🏗️ Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment:** Vercel (free tier)
- **Analytics:** Vercel Analytics
- **Contact:** WhatsApp link

## 🎨 Brand Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: Blue (trust, professional)
- Secondary: Green (growth, success)
- Neutral: Slate/Gray

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold, clear hierarchy
- Body: Readable, 16px+

**Tone:**
- Friendly but professional
- Indonesian language (casual tapi credible)
- No jargon

---

## 📱 Responsive Breakpoints

- Mobile: 320px - 640px
- Tablet: 641px - 1024px
- Desktop: 1025px+

- Load time < 3s
